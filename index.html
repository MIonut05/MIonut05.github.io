About

A+ Sunsets is a small gallery app for people who stop what they're doing when the sky turns. Save a photo, tag where you were, and keep every sky you've caught in one place instead of losing them in a camera roll of ten thousand other things.

Our story

It started in 2022 with a shared album between friends who kept texting each other photos of the same sunset from different streets. The album got too big, so we built something better. Four years later it's an app, launching August 1, 2026.
